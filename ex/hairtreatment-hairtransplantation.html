Hair Transplantation

HAIR TRANSPLANTATION: FOLLICULAR UNIT EXTRACTION (F.U.E.)/ FOLLICULAR UNIT TRANSPLANTATION (F.U.T.)

F.U.E is Follicular Unit Extraction hair transplantation methodology that is popular globally. This procedure is minimally invasive that does not need any admission into a clinic where it is performed. Here, follicular unit grafts, with a few hair follicles, are separately extracted from the innately tougher areas of the scalp of a person and cautiously implanted in the area where hair is missing.

In F.U.T (Follicular unit transplantation methodology), the surgeon designated for the procedure extracts a strip of donor skin to collect distinct follicular units to be transplanted in the areas of hair loss.

WHAT IS HAIR TRANSPLANTATION?

It is a surgical procedure performed by a plastic surgeon or a dermatological surgeon (Dermato Surgeon). A designated surgeon removes hair from an extracted area which has enough hair follicles to a bald area of the head and implants. The surgeon usually moves hair from the back or side of the head to the front or top of the head. Hair transplantation is performed under strict supervision of our experts after local anesthesia (Tumescent) and regional blocks is administered.

Hair loss occurs due to various factors like aging, genetics, etc. Other reasons are:

• Poor Diet

• Extreme stress

• Prolonged illness

• Hormonal imbalance

• Prolonged medications

Eyebrows, mustache area and beard area are also amenable for hair transplantation.

Pre-surgery evaluation/pre-medications will qualify the patient for fitness for hair transplantation.

POST TREATMENT

This is primarily an outpatient procedure; so, you can go home immediately once the transplantation procedure is completed.

Your normal life is not impacted and you can continue to carry on with your usual activities. The only care you need to take is to stay away from external world for a few weeks, so that you can protect the transplanted area from sun light or any other harsh external factors.

ARE THERE ANY POTENTIAL SIDE EFFECTS?

After the transplantation procedure is performed, your scalp may feel sore and swollen. Some antibiotics are prescribed to stay clear from any inflammation or pain. The total healing process may take a few weeks before a person can start feeling normal.
